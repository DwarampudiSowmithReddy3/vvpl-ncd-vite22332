HOW VARIABLES WORK NOW:
------------------------
Variables are embedded directly in templates!

Example Template:
"Dear {InvestorName}, your interest of {Amount}
for {SeriesName} has been processed to account
{BankAccountNumber}. - VVPL"

When sending:
  Backend automatically replaces:
  - {InvestorName} → "Rajesh Kumar"
  - {Amount} → "₹10,000"
  - {SeriesName} → "Series A"
  - {BankAccountNumber} → "XXXX1234"
