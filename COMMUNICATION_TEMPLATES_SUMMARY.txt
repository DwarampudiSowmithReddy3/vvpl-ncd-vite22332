AFTER (Fetched from Database):

- Frontend fetches variables via API: /communication/variables

HOW TO SETUP:
-------------
1. Run: CREATE_COMMUNICATION_TEMPLATES.bat
   (Enter MySQL username and password when prompted)

2. Restart backend:
   - Stop backend (Ctrl+C)
   - Run: START_BACKEND.bat

3. Test in frontend:
   - Go to Communication Center
   - Click "Quick Templates:" dropdown
   - You should see templates from database
