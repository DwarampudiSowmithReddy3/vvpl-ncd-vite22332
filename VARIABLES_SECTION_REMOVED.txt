HOW TO USE VARIABLES:
---------------------
Variables are already in the templates!

When you select a template like:
"Interest Payment Notification"

"Dear {InvestorName}, your interest of {Amount}
for {SeriesName} has been processed to account
{BankAccountNumber}. - VVPL"

The backend will automatically replace:
- {InvestorName} → Actual investor name
- {Amount} → Actual amount
- {SeriesName} → Actual series name
- {BankAccountNumber} → Actual account number
